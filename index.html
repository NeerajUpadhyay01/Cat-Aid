About

  • Shelter and Living Facilities:
    • The center provides a safe and comfortable environment for cats with well-designed living spaces.
    • Individual enclosures or communal areas are set up, ensuring each cat has adequate space and amenities.
  • Veterinary Care:
    • A professional veterinary team oversees the health and medical needs of the cats.
    • Regular check-ups, vaccinations, spaying or neutering, and treatment for any illnesses or injuries are part of the comprehensive healthcare program.
  • Socialization and Enrichment:
    • Trained staff and volunteers engage in socialization activities to ensure cats are comfortable around humans and other cats.
    • Enrichment programs, including toys, scratching posts, and climbing structures, contribute to mental stimulation and physical exercise.
  • Adoption Services:
    • The center actively promotes cat adoptions by facilitating meet-and-greet sessions between potential adopters and cats.
    • Adoption processes include screening, counseling, and follow-up to ensure a suitable match between the cat and its new family.
